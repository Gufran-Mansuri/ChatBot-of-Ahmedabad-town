Q12) name few popular festivals which is celebrated in ahmedabad?
Q12) tell me something about unique festivals of ahmedabad?
A12) Ahmedabad observes a range of festivals. Celebrations and observances include Uttarayan, 
     an annual kite-flying day on 14 and 15 January. Nine nights of Navratri are celebrated

     of Tajia during the Muslim holy month of Muharram are important events.
